Project #2 Protect the Kingdom from Bugs

Protect the kingdom from bugs! Click to squish the bugs before they enter the kindom. Clicking on the bugs will increase your score. Letting bugs into your kingdom will kill your citizens. Highest score wins!

Scratch Project:

Our game made from scracth is a survival based game. You play as the character the bunny as you run through multiple different backgrounds. As you are running through these backgrounds you have to press the spacebar to jump and avoid dangerous spikes. If you hit the spikes you die and the game is over. Every 5 jumps the background changes aswell as your character. There is a score keeper in the top left keeping track how how well and how long you are staying alive.
